The Reform Cycle

Conversations on education's past and future.

Over the past sixty years, education has carried some enormous promises: universal schooling, education for development, Education for All. The Reform Cycle returns to the people who made and lived those promises, from the architects and officials to the teachers and students, to ask what they believed was possible, what stood in the way and what remains of it now. Each conversation looks back in order to see the present more clearly.
